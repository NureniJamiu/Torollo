pricing faq

The fine print, in plain words.

When can I actually pay?

When cloud save and sharing ship. Waitlist members hear first and get the early-bird lifetime offer before it closes.

Will the Free plan get worse when paid plans launch?

No. Everything free today stays free — it’s MIT-licensed open source running on your machine. Paid plans only cover new services that run on our servers.

What exactly does Lifetime include?

All Pro features for life, for a one-time 149€. It’s a limited launch offer to back development early; it will be withdrawn once the service is stable.

How is Education priced?

Per class or per seat, shaped with each pilot partner. If you run a bootcamp, school course, or internal training program, book a pilot and we’ll scope it together.
